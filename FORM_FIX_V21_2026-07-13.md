# VIPACH form fix v21

## Root causes corrected

1. The Content Security Policy allowed form submission to `script.google.com`, but did not allow the hidden iframe to navigate to the Google Apps Script response and its `script.googleusercontent.com` redirect.
2. The iframe used the HTML `hidden` attribute and could be unreliable in Safari. It is now a real 1×1 off-screen iframe.
3. A reused iframe could fire its initial `about:blank` load event as if the POST had completed. Every submission now creates a fresh iframe and ignores the initial load.
4. Form attributes are restored after submission and the temporary iframe is removed.
5. All three language pages retain their own verified endpoint mapping.
