# Elon Musk-style audit — VIPACH model project

## Result
The page was simplified around one proposition: a selective art project with a clear physical outcome. Ambiguous price framing, incomplete legal links and JavaScript-only language SEO were corrected.

## High-impact changes
- Real HU/EN/DE crawlable routes instead of relying only on runtime translation.
- Clear value architecture: estimated total project value EUR 1,200; participant contribution EUR 299 at material/production cost.
- Four deliverables visible above the fold of the offer section, including the champagne vernissage.
- Lenticular artwork explained as a physical optical print, not a vague effect.
- Contract formation separated from application submission.
- Privacy checkbox changed from unnecessary bundled consent to acknowledgement; pre-contractual processing uses Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
- Real localized legal pages and withdrawal model.
- Organization, brand, curator, service, offer, location and image graph in JSON-LD.
- Sitemap, robots, hreflang, canonical, GEO tags, llms.txt and security headers.

## Remaining owner decisions
- Confirm the exact legal contracting/account-holder entity and whether the participation amount is subject to or exempt from VAT.
- The exact approved Adobe Firefly concept image is included separately and used responsively without cropping; the page clearly states that it is an illustration, not a real gallery, and not the project’s actual venue.
- Add the exact November 2026 exhibition date and venue once contractually secured; until then schema deliberately does not publish an invented Event date.
- Have the final model/exhibition release reviewed separately; it should not be bundled into general marketing consent.

- Lentikuláris GIF-példa: hozzájárulás-alapú, kattintásra betöltődő Imgur embed és közvetlen forráslink mindhárom nyelven.
- Google Apps Script: a három eredeti endpoint változatlan; a mellékelt kód az eredeti logikát őrzi, 299 EUR-os és bécsi galériás szövegpontosítással.
